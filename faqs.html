Become a Homeowner

Foster lets you own a home without the exorbitant prices associated with typical mortgages. 

Invest in the Housing Market

Build your wealth with real estate at a reasonable price. Foster lets you join the market without the responsibilities of home ownership

How Does Foster Work?

When you decide to join us as a home owner, we go through some due diligence to determine an adequate budget to shop with. Once you select your dream home, we work with lenders to get an appropriate mortgage. Joining as an investor lets you buy fractional shares in eligible houses.

For Home Buyers

Foster brings together home buyers and everyday investors to create the most optimal situation. Home buyers can buy their dream home (with a standard 20% down payment). The remaining amount is paid through potential investors, who take a share of the home's value proportional to their investment. 

As a result of the home buying process, we require you to continue owning the home for 5 years after the home buying process. After this period, you can sell your home, distributing excess profits to investors.

After determining a set budget, we refer you to our preferred lenders. Our goal is to ease your stress during the mortgage application process.

Only the buyer of the home has rights to use the home, investors have no occupancy rights to the home.

For Investors

With Foster Estates, our home buyers are always on the titles of homes and possess 100% of the occupancy rights. Investors and home buyers form a co-ownership agreement, ensuring buyers have occupancy rights and investors are entitled to a share of the property.
